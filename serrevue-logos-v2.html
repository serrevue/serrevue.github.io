Brand · Logo exploration · v2

Six fresh marks for serrevue.

kept   type system + warm palette new   concepts, none repeated from v1 test   favicon · mono · reversed

A second pass that holds onto what you liked — Space Grotesk + Fraunces italic and the sage / cream / honey palette — but reaches for new ideas: the daily rhythm of a farm, voice, glass, water, and guidance.

Each mark is drawn to survive a 16-pixel favicon, reverse cleanly on a dark field, and hold in a single colour. Honey shows up only as a secondary accent inside the mark, never alone.

01AUROREsunrise + rows
serrevue

The morning briefing, as a logo.

A sun rising over field rows. It nods at the product's best habit — the daily summary waiting for you at dawn — and reads instantly as growing season. Honey sun, sage rows.
02POUSSE·Vmonogram + sprout
serrevue

The “V” of vue, growing a leaf.

A confident V — for vue, and for a young shoot pushing up from a seed — that buds a single leaf off its right arm. The most ownable as a standalone letter for app icons and merch.
03VOIXvoice + crop rows
serrevue

A voice waveform that is also a field.

Five bars rising from a furrow line: read it as the sound of you talking to your greenhouse, or as crops standing in a row. Animates beautifully when the mic is listening.
04CARREAUXglass + sprout
serrevue

A pane of greenhouse glass.

The most literal nod to serre: a window divided into panes, one of which is quietly growing a sprout. Architectural and calm — strong on signage and the side of a building.
05GOUTTEwater + leaf
serrevue

A drop of water with a leaf inside.

Irrigation is half of what SERREVUE controls. The dewdrop carries a negative-space leaf — water and growth in one shape, and a hint of a lens for the vue.
06BOUSSOLEcompass + leaf
serrevue

A north star that's also a leaf.

SERREVUE is the operator that points the way. A four-point compass star with a leaf vein down its axis — guidance and growth. Reads as confident and a little premium.
