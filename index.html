About SerreVue
About SerreVue

The software layer that runs a greenhouse.

SerreVue is an AI operator for commercial greenhouses. It connects to the sensors, cameras, climate computers and irrigation you already run, builds a live digital twin of the greenhouse, and lets a grower manage the whole operation by talking to it in plain language, in English or in French.

A grower spends hours every week on decisions a machine can make continuously and better: when to vent, when to shade, when to irrigate, when a pest or disease risk is building. SerreVue takes that load, keeps every action inside the limits the grower sets, and writes down what it did and why.

01

Sense

We pull live climate, soil, water and camera data out of the hardware you already own into one live model of the greenhouse. No rip and replace.

02

Reason

The AI operator reads that data against your crop plan and the weather ahead, explains what it is seeing in plain language, and proposes the next move.

03

Act

It moves vents, shade, fans, lights and irrigation through a mandatory policy gate. Every action stays inside your rules, and every action is logged for you to review.

The problem

A crop can go in a single night.

Greenhouse conditions turn faster than a person can watch them. These are thresholds every grower already knows, and the hours you get to catch them.

1 night
Freeze
A boiler failing overnight in deep winter, with no backup, can take a whole crop before morning.
8-12 h
Botrytis
Continuous leaf wetness above 93% RH is all Botrytis needs to infect and take a house.
72 h
Powdery mildew
Mildew can reproduce in as little as three days, so a missed lesion becomes an epidemic.
1000x
pH drift
A media pH drop from 6.0 to 5.0 raises iron and manganese solubility roughly a thousandfold.
1 thrip
Tospovirus
When tospovirus is present the action threshold falls from about fifteen per card to a single insect.
None of these wait for business hours. SerreVue watches every zone continuously and acts before a threshold becomes a loss, always inside the rules you set.
Sources: Alabama, MSU, UMass, NC State, PNW, UGA, OSU, Rutgers and USU Extension.
