01 · The idea

One silhouette that reads as both a leaf and a chat bubble — with a typing wink inside.

  • 1 Apex top-right. A clean pointed corner anchors the design on the diagonal — the leaf tip, where it would meet a branch.
  • 2 Asymmetric body. Fuller on the right, narrower on the left. Reads as a real leaf, not a balloon.
  • 3 Petiole as chat tail. The leaf's natural stem kicks down-and-left and becomes the bubble's tail. One shape, two readings.
  • 4 Three dots inside. Read as a “typing…” indicator in chat contexts, and as seeds or droplets in leaf contexts. Gracefully disappears at favicon size, leaving the silhouette intact.
