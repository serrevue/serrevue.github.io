SERREVUE · primary mark

A leaf, a chat bubble, a “typing…” wink.

One plump leaf with a pointed tip. Its petiole, the little stem, drops to the bottom-left exactly where a chat bubble's tail would be. Three dots sit in the middle: a “typing…” indicator if you read it as a bubble, seeds if you read it as a leaf.

  • 1Pointed apex at the top-right so it reads as a leaf first.
  • 2Rounded stem at the bottom-left: a petiole and a bubble tail in one.
  • 3Three dots in the centre. They drop out gracefully at favicon size, leaving a clean silhouette.
